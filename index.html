How Georgia TAVT Works for Leases (HB 63, eff. Jan 2022) Georgia charges a one-time TAVT instead of annual vehicle property tax. For leases, TAVT is calculated on the depreciation amount plus any capitalized fees (Adjusted Cap Cost − Residual Value). The rent charge (finance fee) is excluded from the tax base. There is no additional monthly sales tax on your payment. Down payment does not affect TAVT.
